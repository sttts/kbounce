# Privacy Policy for KBounce

Last updated: January 2026

KBounce ("the Game") is developed by Stefan Schimanski.

## Data Collection

The Game collects minimal data for the online leaderboard feature:
- Nickname (optional, you choose what to display)
- Game score and level
- Approximate location (country/city, derived from IP address)
- Screenshot of completed levels (optional)

This data is stored on our servers to display the global leaderboard.

## Data Not Collected

We do not collect:
- Personal information (name, email, phone)
- Device identifiers
- Usage analytics or tracking data

## Data Retention

Leaderboard entries are retained indefinitely. You may request deletion by contacting kbounce@1stein.org.
